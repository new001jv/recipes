Ingredients:
	- Package of chicken tenders
	- 2 to 3 chopped potatos
	- 1 chopped onion
	- 5 garlic cloves, diced
	- 2 table spoons currey
	- Can of coconut milk
	- 2 table spoons of butter
	- olive oil

Directions:
 1. Sautee the onions and garlic in olive oil and butter
 2. Warm the milk and currey in sauce pan
 3. Slightly cook the chicken with the onions and garlic.
 4. Add the sauce and potatos.
 5. Cook for 30 minutes
